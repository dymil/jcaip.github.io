## Points, Lines, Vectors, Planes
A **linear combination** of $$m$$ vectors is given by 
$$ \textbf{w} = a_1\textbf{v_1} + \ldots + a_m\textbf{v_m} $$

An **affince combination** is a linear combination such that
$$ \sum{a_i}_{i=1}^{m} = 1$$
